Protein Requirements

Proteins are essential for building and repairing muscles, making them a crucial part of your fitness nutrition. Here are some guidelines for protein intake:

  • Daily Protein Intake: Aim for 10-35% of your daily calories to come from protein.
  • Body Weight: Calculate your protein needs based on your body weight and fitness goals.
  • Lean Sources: Choose lean protein sources like chicken, turkey, fish, lean beef, tofu, and legumes.
  • Timing: Distribute your protein intake throughout the day to support muscle protein synthesis.
  • Post-Workout: Consume protein after workouts to aid muscle recovery.
  • Supplementation: Consider protein supplements like whey or plant-based protein if needed.

Remember to consult a professional or nutritionist to determine your specific protein needs for your fitness goals.
