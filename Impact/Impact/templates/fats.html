Fat Requirements

Fats are an essential part of a balanced diet for fitness and overall health. Here's some guidance on fat intake for fitness purposes:

  • Daily Fat Intake: Aim for 20-35% of your daily calories to come from healthy fats.
  • Types of Fats: Focus on unsaturated fats like those found in nuts, seeds, avocados, and olive oil.
  • Avoid Trans Fats: Limit or avoid trans fats found in many processed and fried foods.
  • Saturated Fats: Limit saturated fats found in red meat, dairy, and tropical oils.
  • Essential Fatty Acids: Include omega-3 and omega-6 fatty acids from sources like fatty fish, flaxseeds, and walnuts.
  • Fat Timing: Include healthy fats in your meals to support overall health, but keep them in moderation due to their calorie density.

Remember that individual dietary needs can vary, so consult with a nutritionist or dietitian to determine the right fat intake for your fitness goals and overall well-being.
