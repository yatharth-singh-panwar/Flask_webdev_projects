Vitamins and Minerals Requirements

Vitamins and minerals are essential for overall health and play a significant role in supporting fitness goals. Here are some guidelines:

Vitamins:

  • Vitamin A: Supports vision and immune function. Sources include carrots, sweet potatoes, and spinach.
  • Vitamin C: Important for collagen production and immune support. Found in citrus fruits and bell peppers.
  • Vitamin D: Aids in calcium absorption and bone health. Sunlight and fortified foods are sources.

Minerals:

  • Calcium: Essential for strong bones and muscle function. Dairy products, leafy greens, and almonds are good sources.
  • Magnesium: Supports muscle and nerve function. Found in nuts, seeds, and whole grains.
  • Potassium: Helps maintain electrolyte balance. Bananas, potatoes, and beans are high in potassium.

Consult with a nutritionist to determine your specific vitamin and mineral needs based on your fitness goals and overall health.
